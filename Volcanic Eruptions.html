VOLCANIC ERUPTIONS

When magma is released from a volcano, it causes a volcanic eruption. An eruption of molten rock, hot rock fragments, and hot gases from a volcano, which is a vent in the crust of a planet or satellite. Volcanic eruptions are among the most dramatic and violent change agents.They can harm the ecosystem, the climate, and the health of those who are exposed, as well as deteriorating social and economic conditions.

Lava flows are rivers of flowing or oozing molten rock that originate from an eruption vent. Either nonexplosive activities or explosive lava fountains cause lava to flow.
