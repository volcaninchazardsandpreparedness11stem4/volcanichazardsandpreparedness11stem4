What are these?

Positives

  • Volcanic ash and rock provide fertile land, resulting in higher crop yields for farmers.

  • Tourists flock to the volcano, bringing more money into the local economy.

  • Geothermal energy can be harnessed, providing locals with free electricity.

  • Minerals, such as diamonds, are found in lava and can be mined for profit.

Negatives

  • Volcanoes are extremely dangerous. They have the potential to kill people and cause property damage.

  • Economic activity may suffer because it is difficult for businesses to operate following an eruption.

  • Lava flows destroy habitats and landscapes.

How do Volcanoes Erupt?

  • Deep beneath the Earth, the temperature is so high that certain rocks slowly melt and turn into magma, a viscous fluid. Magma rises and gathers in magma chambers because it is lighter than the surrounding solid rock. Some of the magma eventually makes its way to the Earth's surface via cracks and vents, resulting in a volcanic eruption.
